About me

I'm currently working at DGene as a senior research engineer. I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Louisiana State University, under the guidance of Dr. Mingxuan Sun. My research focuses on developing computationally efficient machine learning as well as deep learning models and algorithms, with applications in recommender systems, time-series analysis, computer vision, computer graphics, and natural language processing.
